Our Mission

The story behind the sound.

Founded in 2021 by a team of linguistic experts and deep-learning engineers, Vistrow started with a simple observation: most global AI models failed to capture the complexity of Indian dialects.

We spent years building proprietary datasets and training models specifically for the Indian linguistic landscape, from Hinglish code-switching to regional phonetic variations.

Today, we help the world's largest brands talk to India.
